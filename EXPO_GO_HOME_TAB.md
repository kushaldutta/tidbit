# Connecting from Expo Go Home Tab

## You're in the Right Place!

The Expo Go app on your phone has:
- **Home** tab
- **Diagnostics** tab  
- **Settings** tab

## How to Connect:

### Method 1: Home Tab - Look for Connection Options

1. **Tap the "Home" tab** (should already be selected)
2. **Look for:**
   - A **search bar** at the top
   - A **"Enter URL"** or **"Connect"** button
   - A **"+"** button (might be floating or in top right)
   - **"Scan QR code"** option
   - Any text input field

3. **If you see a search/input field**, enter:
   ```
   exp://10.0.0.8:8081
   ```

### Method 2: Try Swiping or Long Press

- **Swipe down** on the Home screen - might reveal a URL input
- **Long press** on the Home screen - might show connection options
- **Tap and hold** anywhere - might bring up a menu

### Method 3: Check Settings Tab

1. **Tap "Settings"** tab
2. **Look for:**
   - "Connect to Server"
   - "Development Server"
   - "Enter URL"
   - Any connection-related options

### Method 4: Try the Diagnostics Tab

1. **Tap "Diagnostics"** tab
2. Sometimes connection options are here for troubleshooting

## What to Look For:

On the **Home tab**, you should see one of these:
- A search bar at the top
- A "+" or "Add" button
- "Recent Projects" section (might be empty)
- "Enter URL manually" option
- QR code scanner option
